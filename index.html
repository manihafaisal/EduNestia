About MyEduSite

MyEduSite is a simple hub to organize your study materials by subject. Add books (PDFs) and lectures (slides) into subject folders, and the site automatically presents them neatly with filters for quick access.

How it works

  • Choose a subject from the Courses page.
  • Switch between Books and Lectures tabs.
  • Click any card to open the file (PDF/PPTX/etc.).

Subjects covered

  • Physics, Calculus, Programming
  • Pakistan Studies, Ideology, Functional English
  • Computer Science, Web Development, Databases

Tips

  • Place files inside the subject folders (e.g., physics_content/).
  • Use clear filenames (avoid very long names when possible).
  • Keep video links and short notes on each resource in the data list if needed.
